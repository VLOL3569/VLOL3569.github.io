About Me

I'm a 2nd year student studying Integrated Biomedical Engineering and Health Sciences at McMaster University. My interest lies in leveraging emerging technologies to enhance healthcare outcomes.

Aside from my academic pursuits, I enjoy volunteering, playing basketball, exploring nature, and music! I am always eager to meet new people and collaborate on exciting projects, so please reach out!

Out of over 50 participating teams, we won the annual INNOVATX case competition organized by Médecins Sans Frontières by proposing the implementation of Wolffia globosa, a novel and promising food source, in Yemen. Our abstract was later published in the URNCST journal.

Hip Implant Design

We designed a new hip implant in AutoDesk Inventor for a 51-year old total hip arthroplasty patient suffering from aseptic loosening from previous implant. We presented our project at the iBioMed Fall Showcase.

Novel Therapeautic R&D

Me and my IBEHS 1EP6 team members designed a novel AAV-based gene therapy a TGF-β pathway to reduce intraocular pressure in glaucoma. Using BioRender to map out the pathways in our proof-of-concept, we created a research presentation and showcased our proposal to professors and peers.

BioRender

Protein Bioinformatics Project

I performed SDS-PAGE gel electrophoresis on actin samples from five aquatic species to construct a cladogram that represented the evolutionary relationships between the species. Using BioPython to access amino acid sequences from the NCBI database, I computed the percent identity between each species.
